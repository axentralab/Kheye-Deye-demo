Our Story

A journey of passion, heritage, and unforgettable flavors — from a family kitchen to a city icon.

🍲
Since 2015

Born from Grandmother's Kitchen

Khey Deye started as a humble dream: to share the authentic tastes of Bangladesh with everyone. Our founder Karim Uddin grew up watching his grandmother cook elaborate feasts for the family, where every dish told a story of love, patience, and tradition.

In 2015, Karim opened the first Khey Deye outlet in Dhanmondi. Within a year, word had spread across Dhaka. Today, we serve over 2,000 happy guests daily and deliver across the city — with the same soul that started in that small kitchen.

Our vision is simple: great food should be accessible to everyone. Every ingredient is locally sourced, every recipe is authentic, and every meal is made with genuine care.
